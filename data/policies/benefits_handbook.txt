2. FINANCIAL BENEFITS
----------------------

2.1 Provident Fund (PF)
- Employee Contribution: 12% of basic salary
- Employer Contribution: 12% of basic salary
- Withdrawal: As per PF rules
- Interest: As per government rates

2.2 Gratuity
- Eligibility: After 5 years of service
- Calculation: 15 days salary for each completed year
- Payment: At retirement or resignation

2.3 Performance Bonus
- Eligibility: All employees after probation
- Frequency: Annual
- Amount: Based on individual and company performance
- Range: 0-30% of annual salary
- Payment: March of each year

2.4 Referral Bonus
- Amount: ₹25,000 for successful referral
- Eligibility: After referred candidate completes 6 months
- No limit on number of referrals

2.5 Relocation Assistance
- One-time payment: ₹50,000
- Eligibility: Employees relocating for job
- Temporary accommodation: 15 days

2.6 Meal Allowance
- Daily meal vouchers: ₹150
- Applicable for office working days
- Can be used at partner restaurants
