1. BEFORE YOUR FIRST DAY
------------------------

Congratulations on joining our team! Here's what to expect before you start:

1.1 Pre-Joining Formalities
- Document Submission (7 days before joining):
  * Educational certificates
  * Previous employment letters
  * Address proof
  * PAN card, Aadhar card
  * Passport size photos (3)
  * Bank account details
  * PF transfer form (if applicable)

1.2 Background Verification
- Typically takes 5-7 business days
- You'll receive confirmation via email
- Contact HR if you have questions: onboarding@company.com

1.3 What to Expect
- Welcome email with joining details
- IT equipment assignment
- Buddy assignment (your go-to person for first 3 months)
- First week schedule

1.4 Dress Code
- Business Casual: Monday to Thursday
- Casual: Friday (Smart casuals, no shorts/flip-flops)
- Formal: Client meetings or special events

4. FIRST MONTH GOALS
--------------------

Week 1: Orientation & Setup
- Complete all administrative tasks
- Understand company culture and values
- Meet your immediate team
- Set up development environment

Week 2: Learning
- Shadow team members
- Review past projects
- Understand workflows and processes
- Complete assigned training modules
- Start small tasks

Week 3: Contributing
- Take ownership of first project/task
- Participate actively in meetings
- Ask questions and seek feedback
- Build relationships across teams

Week 4: Integration
- Deliver first project/task
- Receive and act on feedback
- Identify areas for improvement
- Set goals for next 60 days

5. 90-DAY ONBOARDING PLAN
--------------------------

Month 1: Learn
- Understand company, team, and role
- Build foundational knowledge
- Establish relationships
- Complete initial training
- Deliver small tasks successfully

Month 2: Contribute
- Take on meaningful projects
- Work more independently
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams
- Seek and apply feedback
- Identify improvement opportunities

Month 3: Excel
- Own significant deliverables
- Demonstrate expertise in key areas
- Mentor newer team members
- Contribute to team strategy
- Complete probation review

5.1 Check-in Schedule
- Week 1: Daily check-ins with buddy
- Week 2-4: Weekly 1-on-1 with manager
- Month 2-3: Bi-weekly 1-on-1 with manager
- Day 90: Formal probation review

7. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
------------------------------

Q1: When will I receive my first salary?
A: Salary is processed on the last working day of the month. If you join mid-month, 
you'll receive pro-rated salary for that month.

Q2: How do I apply for leave?
A: Use the HR portal to submit leave requests. Ensure manager approval before 
making travel plans.

Q3: What are the working hours?
A: Core hours are 11 AM - 4 PM. You can choose flexible start (8-11 AM) and 
end times (4-8 PM) for a total of 9 hours including lunch.

Q4: Can I work from home?
A: Yes, 2 days per week with manager approval. Submit WFH requests through 
the HR portal.

Q5: When can I enroll in health insurance?
A: You can enroll from Day 1. Complete the enrollment form and submit to HR 
within 30 days of joining.

Q6: How do I access the office WiFi?
A: IT team will provide credentials on Day 1. Network name: CompanySecure

Q7: Is there a dress code?
A: Business casual Monday-Thursday, casual Friday. Formals for client meetings.

Q8: How do I book meeting rooms?
A: Use Outlook/Google Calendar to check availability and book rooms.

Q9: What if I'm sick on a working day?
A: Inform your manager as soon as possible and apply for sick leave through 
the HR portal.

Q10: Who do I contact for IT issues?
A: Email it@company.com or call Ext: 1234. For urgent issues, visit IT desk 
on 2nd floor.

Q11: When is the probation review?
A: Formal probation review happens at 90 days (3 months). You'll have regular 
check-ins with your manager throughout.

Q12: Can I refer someone for a job opening?
A: Yes! We have a referral program with â‚¹25,000 bonus for successful hires. 
Check open positions on the careers page.

Q13: How do I claim reimbursements?
A: Submit expense reports through the HR portal with proper bills/receipts. 
Reimbursements are processed within 7 working days.

Q14: What training is mandatory?
A: Information Security, Code of Conduct, and role-specific training must be 
completed in the first month.

Q15: How can I provide feedback?
A: We encourage open communication. Talk to your manager, buddy, or HR team. 
Anonymous feedback can be submitted through the HR portal.
