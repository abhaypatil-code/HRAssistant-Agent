COMPANY LEAVE POLICY
====================

Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Version: 2.0

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
This document outlines the leave policy for all employees of the company. All employees are entitled to various types of leave as described below.

2. TYPES OF LEAVE
-----------------

2.1 Casual Leave (CL)
- Entitlement: 15 days per year
- Purpose: Personal matters, emergencies, or short breaks
- Advance Notice: Minimum 2 days notice required (except emergencies)
- Carry Forward: Maximum 5 days can be carried to next year
- Encashment: Not eligible for encashment

2.2 Sick Leave (SL)
- Entitlement: 12 days per year
- Purpose: Medical illness or health-related issues
- Medical Certificate: Required for leaves exceeding 3 consecutive days
- Carry Forward: Maximum 10 days can be carried to next year
- Encashment: Not eligible for encashment

2.3 Earned Leave (EL) / Privilege Leave
- Entitlement: Based on tenure
  * 0-2 years: 15 days per year
  * 2-5 years: 20 days per year
  * 5+ years: 25 days per year
- Advance Notice: Minimum 7 days notice required
- Carry Forward: Maximum 30 days can be carried forward
- Encashment: Eligible for encashment at year-end (max 15 days)

2.4 Maternity Leave
- Entitlement: 26 weeks (182 days) for first two children
- Entitlement: 12 weeks for third child onwards
- Eligibility: Female employees who have worked for at least 80 days
- Pay: Full pay during maternity leave
- Work from Home: Optional 2 months WFH after leave period
- Advance Notice: Minimum 8 weeks before expected delivery date

2.5 Paternity Leave
- Entitlement: 15 days
- Eligibility: Male employees for first two children
- Timing: Can be taken within 6 months of child's birth
- Pay: Full pay during paternity leave
- Advance Notice: Minimum 2 weeks notice

2.6 Bereavement Leave
- Entitlement: 5 days for immediate family members
- Immediate Family: Spouse, children, parents, siblings
- Pay: Full pay
- Notice: Inform manager as soon as possible

2.7 Marriage Leave
- Entitlement: 7 days
- Eligibility: Once during employment tenure
- Advance Notice: Minimum 1 month notice
- Documentation: Marriage certificate required

3. LEAVE APPLICATION PROCESS
-----------------------------

3.1 How to Apply
- Submit leave request through HR portal or email to manager
- Include: Leave type, dates, reason, contact information
- Await approval before making travel arrangements

3.2 Approval Process
- Manager approval required for all leaves
- HR approval required for leaves exceeding 7 days
- Emergency leaves can be applied retrospectively within 24 hours

3.3 Leave Cancellation
- Approved leaves can be cancelled with manager consent
- Cancellation must be done at least 24 hours before leave start date

4. LEAVE BALANCE TRACKING
--------------------------
- Employees can check leave balance through HR portal
- Monthly leave balance statements sent via email
- Leave balance resets on January 1st each year (except carry-forward eligible leaves)

5. UNPAID LEAVE
---------------
- Available when all paid leaves are exhausted
- Requires VP approval
- Affects salary and benefits calculation
- Maximum 30 days per year

6. SABBATICAL LEAVE
-------------------
- Entitlement: Up to 6 months
- Eligibility: Employees with 5+ years of service
- Purpose: Education, personal development, family care
- Pay: Unpaid
- Approval: Requires CEO approval
- Job Protection: Position guaranteed upon return

7. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
------------------
- Company observes 12 public holidays per year
- List published at beginning of each year
- Employees working on public holidays receive compensatory off

8. SPECIAL PROVISIONS
---------------------

8.1 Medical Emergencies
- Extended sick leave available with medical documentation
- Company health insurance covers hospitalization
- Manager and HR must be informed immediately

8.2 Remote Work During Leave
- Not permitted unless specifically approved
- Violating this policy may result in leave cancellation

8.3 Leave During Notice Period
- Only earned leave can be availed during notice period
- Subject to manager approval and business requirements

9. POLICY VIOLATIONS
--------------------
- Unauthorized absence treated as leave without pay
- False information in leave application may lead to disciplinary action
- Excessive unplanned leaves may affect performance review
